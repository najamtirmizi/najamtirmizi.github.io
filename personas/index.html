Which misfit persona are you?

Name your pain—Tame your pain

Pick the tile that stings most—
you’ll instantly know your growth path.

The Quiet Beacon

“When I try to explain what I do, people still don’t get it.”

You have a powerful craft and a head full of ideas, but the signal gets lost in translation. Together we’ll clarify your voice, tighten your copy, and turn every intro or About page into an instant “aha!” moment for the right people.

  • “I don’t know how to describe what I offer in a sentence.”
  • “My branding is inconsistent and doesn’t reflect who I am.”
  • “I keep rewriting my website copy but still hate how it sounds.”

The Misfit Magnet

“I’m attracting clients who aren’t my people.”

Your vibe is unique, but your tribe hasn’t found you—yet. We’ll refine your audience filter, realign offers to match your best‑fit buyers, and give you practical tactics to build a community that energizes instead of drains.

  • “I keep attracting the wrong kind of clients.”
  • “I want to pivot, but I’m afraid of alienating my audience.”
  • “I’ve outgrown my existing audience.”

The Hesitant Closer

“I freeze or feel guilty the moment I must talk price.”

You believe in the value you create, but money conversations trigger a full stop. We’ll untangle pricing from self‑worth, script confident sales moments, and design a no‑pressure path that lets both you and your clients breathe easier.

  • “I hate doing sales calls and always feel awkward.”
  • “I feel guilty asking for money, even when I’ve delivered value.”
  • “I worry I’m charging too much—
    or too little.”

The Overloaded Operator

“My backend is a spaghetti mess I dread opening.”

The vision is crystal‑clear but the workflows, files, and tools are tangled. We’ll audit, streamline, and automate so your business engine hums in the background while you stay front‑and‑center on meaningful work.

  • “My inbox and files are a chaotic mess.”
  • “Every time I open a task tool, I feel overwhelmed.”
  • “I get stuck when trying to create systems or SOPs.”

The Compass Drifter

“I’m not sure what I’m building — or why.”

Ideas come easily, but a clear direction does not. We’ll surface your true “north star,” stress‑test big decisions, and map an actionable roadmap so you move forward with confidence instead of spinning in circles.

  • “I don’t know if this idea is even worth pursuing.”
  • “I’m stuck between two niches and don’t know which one to pick.”
  • “I’m building a product I no longer believe in.”

The Guarded Achiever

“Success or exposure feels unsafe — I keep pulling back.”

Growth is happening, yet every new spotlight triggers a pull‑back. We’ll set healthy boundaries, protect your energy, and help you step into visibility without sacrificing well‑being or values.

  • “I’m scared of being copied or ripped off.”
  • “I’m stuck in freebie hell—
    everyone wants advice but no one pays.”
  • “I’m emotionally drained from handling customer support.”
